About Us
Who We Are
We are a group of Individuals that have comes together to collectively help and progress towards a brighter tomorrow, a tomorrow where even those with mental learning disabilities, mental retardation, and illness' that harm every day life can find for themselves, a place in society.
What We Do
We help your family members, friends, and associates who have been gifted with special needs find a brighter future through our services. We have a great team of workers who are ready for your needs at any hour of the day. Through our app, or through our email/Phone you can contact one of our associates and get to finding the future you wish to live in.
How We Do It
We have a multitude of ways to help your loved one obtain the attention they need. From in-person lessons and or talks, to medical assistence, to our app which can help your loved one in day to day activities. Our app serves its users a great user-friendly experience with a chalk-full of features that help your loved one plan their day and contact assistance whenever needed.
Our Vision
At ProjectSigma we aspire for a society that won't turn a blind eye to the mentally challenged. Those who are mentally challenged are often forgotten, stripped from their rights and treated as a burden to the community. We view a future where they are given equal opportunities just like anyone else, not treated differently than us and given facilities by the community. Any step towards helping the mentally challenged aims towards a better society for everyone.

User Friendly

Track Everything, and contain Your Day
We know that for some of our users, an App that reminds them of what they want to do is very useful. Which is why we provide you a seamlessly easy and comfortable day manager built right in.

Control Music, See your Progress, Review your To Do List, and your current goal. Track your overall weekly, monthly, and yearly progress all in the app.

Our intuitive design allows for the implementation of widgets, allowing you to make your own layout for how you wish to see your personal Manager.

Adding things is also easy, use our personal AI Assistant to easily Add, Remove, or Manage your Manager.
